Understanding the Web Study

The Understanding the Web Shield study helps Mozilla know more about your knowledge and opinions of the Internet while you browse. To do so, we have partnered with the Annenberg School for Communication Research at the University of Pennsylvania to conduct a field study on this subject.

You can learn more about the data collection for this study in our Wiki.

How can I report issues?

You can report issues with this study at the following link: https://github.com/raymak/web-health-study/issues

Can I remove this study?

Note that this study is temporary and will be automatically removed after a period of time. If you still want to disable it, click here to see the instructions.

Removing the Study
  1. Open the Add-ons page
  2. Find "Web Health Study" and click the "Remove" button
